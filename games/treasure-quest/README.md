# 🏴‍☠️ Treasure Quest Adventure

An exciting platformer adventure game where you explore ancient worlds, collect treasures, and dodge dangerous monsters!

## 🎮 Features

### 5 Unique Worlds
- 🌲 **Enchanted Forest** - A mystical forest filled with creatures
- 🏜️ **Sandy Desert** - Hot sands with scorpions and traps
- 🕳️ **Dark Cave** - Dark caves with bats and hidden dangers
- 🏰 **Haunted Castle** - Ghosts and spirits lurking in the shadows
- ⛰️ **Mystic Mountain** - The ultimate challenge with dragons

### Gameplay
- **15 Levels** across 5 worlds (3 levels each)
- **Platformer Mechanics** - Run, jump, and dodge
- **Collectibles** - Coins and gems to boost your score
- **Power-ups**:
  - 🛡️ **Shield** - Protects from damage
  - ⚡ **Speed Boost** - Move faster
  - 🧲 **Magnet** - Attract nearby coins
- **Enemies** - Different monsters in each world
- **Health System** - 3 lives with health bars
- **Scoring** - Points, time bonuses, and achievements

### Controls
- **Desktop**:
  - `← →` or `A D` - Move left/right
  - `↑`, `Space`, or `W` - Jump
  - `ESC` - Pause
- **Mobile**: Touch controls on screen

## 🎯 How to Play

1. **Select a World** - Choose from unlocked worlds
2. **Start Adventure** - Click "Start Adventure" to begin
3. **Collect Coins** - Jump and explore to find all coins
4. **Defeat Enemies** - Stomp on enemies from above
5. **Power-ups** - Grab special abilities to help you
6. **Reach the Door** - Find the treasure door to complete each level
7. **Unlock Worlds** - Complete levels to unlock new worlds

## 🏆 Scoring

- **Coin Collection**: +10 points each
- **Enemy Stomp**: +50 points each
- **Time Bonus**: Up to 200 points based on completion time
- **Power-ups**: Temporary abilities to help you score more

## 💾 Progress

- Game automatically saves your unlocked worlds
- Progress is stored in browser localStorage
- Play again to restart from the beginning
